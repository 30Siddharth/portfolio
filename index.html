Hi, I am Siddharth. I am a Ph.D. student in the Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering Department at the University of Virginia currently advised by Prof. Cindy Chang. My research focuses on robotics and learning. The emphasis of my research is to make robots more reliable and safer to operate in industrial environments.

Prior to joining UVA, I completed my M.S.E. from the University of Pennsylvania in Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mechanics(2018). I earned my B.E. in Manufacturing Process & Automation Engineering from NSIT, New Delhi, India(2014). I enjoy playing badminton, cricket and anything related to cars.

Research

I have worked in numerous aspects of robotics and my expertize lies on applied robotics. The aim of my research is to reduce the cost of reprogramming robots and improve their online behaviour with little human supervision.
